📥 Inputs to NBA Engine

  • Customer profile — Demographics, tenure, segment
  • Primacy indicators — Risk signals, behavior changes
  • Current products — What they already have
  • Transaction patterns — Spending categories, amounts
  • Product catalog — Available products, features, rates
  • Eligibility rules — Credit, income, regulatory requirements
  • Historical success — What worked for similar customers

📤 Outputs from NBA Engine

  • Recommended product — Specific product with details
  • Match score — Confidence percentage
  • Reasoning — WHY this product fits this customer
  • Supporting signals — Evidence from customer data
  • Expected value — Revenue/benefit estimate
  • Conversation guide — How to position the offer
  • Alternative options — 2nd and 3rd best recommendations

Research Questions

Key questions for the data science team to explore

🎯 Matching Logic

How do we match customer signals to product benefits? Rule-based? ML? Hybrid?

→ Compare approaches on historical data

📊 Training Data

What historical offer/acceptance data exists? How do we handle cold-start for new products?

→ Data audit + synthetic data strategies

🔢 Ranking

When multiple products fit, how do we rank? Customer value? Bank revenue? Urgency?

→ Multi-objective optimization

💬 Reasoning Quality

How do we ensure reasoning is accurate, specific, and persuasive (not generic)?

→ Human evaluation, A/B testing

⚖️ Fairness

How do we ensure recommendations don't discriminate? Regulatory compliance?

→ Fairness testing, explainability audits

🔄 Timing

When is the right moment to show an NBA? Real-time vs. batch? Trigger-based?

→ Event-driven architecture design

NBA vs. Explainer

How these two GenAI capabilities complement each other

Aspect GenAI Explainer (#1) Next-Best-Action (#2)
Question Answered "What changed and why?" "What should I do about it?"
Output Natural language explanation Product recommendation + reasoning
Complexity Medium — Explain existing data High — Match + Rank + Explain
Dependencies Primacy Indicators, Insights Explainer + Product Catalog + Eligibility
Value Understanding → Trust Action → Revenue
Recommended Order ✅ Build First 🔜 Build Second
