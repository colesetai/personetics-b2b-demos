PHASE 1b

Customer Insights: What It Is

Show bankers the exact same insights that customers see in their mobile banking app

📱 What Customers See

Personalized alerts in mobile app:

  • "Your salary deposit is 5 days late"
  • "You have $28,500 sitting in low-interest savings"
  • "Your auto loan will be paid off in 4 months"
  • "Recurring overdraft risk: balance drops low mid-month"
  • "Your mortgage rate is 2% higher than current rates"

🏦 What Bankers Need

The SAME insights, so they can:

  • Spot life events (job change, cash buildup) for proactive outreach
  • Identify cross-sell opportunities (CD, investment products)
  • Address cash flow issues before they escalate
  • Time conversations around loan payoffs & renewals
  • Reference alerts customers already received — build trust

"I see your auto loan is paid off next quarter — have you thought about what to do with that extra $485/month? Let's talk about building your investment portfolio."
