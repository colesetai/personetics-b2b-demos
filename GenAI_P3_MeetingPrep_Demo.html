What It Does

Auto-generates a personalized meeting brief before every customer interaction

Aggregates

Pulls together data from multiple sources:

  • Customer Snapshot (EDM)
  • Primacy Indicators
  • Recent Insights
  • Previous interactions
  • Open opportunities

Prioritizes

AI identifies what matters most:

  • Risk signals requiring action
  • Opportunities to discuss
  • Changes since last contact
  • Customer's likely concerns
  • Relevant offers to mention

Generates

Creates actionable meeting content:

  • Key alerts summary
  • Financial snapshot
  • 3-5 talking points with CTAs
  • Suggested conversation opener
  • Follow-up action items
