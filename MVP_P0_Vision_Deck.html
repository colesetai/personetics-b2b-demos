MVP Capabilities: Deep Dive

üìä Customer Snapshot + Trends

Why it matters:

Today, bankers spend 15+ minutes gathering customer data from multiple systems. The Snapshot gives them a single-screen view of:

  • Account balances and recent activity
  • Income stability and changes
  • Spending patterns by category
  • Month-over-month trends

Result: Banker understands customer in 15 seconds, not 15 minutes

üîî Insights Mirror

Why it matters:

Customers receive personalized alerts in their mobile app. When they call or visit, bankers should know what they've seen:

  • "I see you received an alert about unusual spending..."
  • "Your income deposit was flagged as lower this month..."
  • "You reached 80% of your savings goal!"

Result: Aligned, contextual conversations that build trust

üìà Primacy Indicators (40+)

Why it matters:

40+ behavioral signals reveal customer relationship health:

  • Salary deposit location & consistency
  • Bill payment patterns (utilities, subscriptions)
  • Transfer patterns (internal vs. external)
  • Balance trends and cash flow

Result: Early warning before customers leave

üè¶ External Relationships

Why it matters:

Detect when customers are using other banks:

  • Transfers to/from competitor accounts
  • External account funding patterns
  • Deposit consolidation opportunities

Result: Identify $187M+ in consolidation opportunities
