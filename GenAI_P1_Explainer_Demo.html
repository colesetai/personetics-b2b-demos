📥 Inputs to GenAI

  • Indicator value — Current state
  • Previous value — What changed
  • Customer context — Segment, tenure, products
  • Transaction details — Supporting evidence
  • Historical patterns — Is this normal for this customer?
  • Benchmark data — How do similar customers behave?

📤 Outputs from GenAI

  • Summary — 1-2 sentence plain language explanation
  • Context — Why this matters (risk/opportunity)
  • Evidence — Supporting data points
  • Probability — Likelihood of churn/conversion
  • CTA Buttons — One-click actions (call, offer, upgrade)
  • Conversation starter — Opening line for customer

Research Questions

Key questions for the data science team to explore

🎯 Accuracy

How do we validate that explanations are factually correct and not hallucinated?

→ Ground truth testing, human evaluation, automated fact-checking

📊 Context Window

How much customer history is needed for quality explanations? 30 days? 90 days? 1 year?

→ Ablation studies on context length vs. quality

⚡ Latency

Can we generate explanations in real-time, or should they be pre-computed?

→ Benchmark response times, caching strategies

🔒 Compliance

How do we ensure explanations are audit-ready and don't expose sensitive reasoning?

→ Explainability requirements, logging, redaction rules

🎨 Tone

What's the right tone? Professional? Conversational? How do we avoid sounding robotic?

→ A/B testing with bankers, tone guidelines

📈 Personalization

Should explanations adapt to banker expertise level or customer segment?

→ Persona-based prompting, configurable detail levels
